Our Aims

What we do

Our organization involves backward, neglected, unemployed women in self-employment and income generating activities

Microcredits for Women

Microcredit is provided with the aim of creating self-employment by engaging trained poor women in productive activities.

Practical Training

Self-employment opportunities are created for women through vocational and practical training at women friendly residential/non-residential training centres.

Entrepreneurship

Women-friendly entrepreneurship development is being conducted by supporting the products and services, marketing and marketing of women entrepreneurs and small organizations scattered all over the country.

Protect Generation

Providing immediate legal assistance on number 10921 to prevent violence against women. Women and children victims of torture can get necessary help from any mobile phone 24 hours a day, including their family members.
